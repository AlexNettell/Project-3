What is
Explorers
Southwest?

Founded and run by Skyler and Finley, Explorers Southwest is a three season (March to October) tour company based in Durango, Colorado, USA, for the mild to mildly adventurous. Their most popular tour, coincidentally the one that started the business, is a guided jeep tour to local "ghost mining towns" in the nearby mountains between the towns of Durango and Silverton. Also on offer is a fully guided day trip tour to the Mesa Verde National Park one hour away. Lastly, they rent stand up paddle boards (SUP), fishing equipment and kayaks to use at the nearby Lake Nighthorse.

Skyler and Finley's Ethos

Skyler and Finley were both born and raised in Colorado. Adventure sports are in their blood. They also understand that they live in a location full of unique, sensitive biodiversity. By taking tourists on guided tours they can not only help reduce the footprint, but also boost the local economy, and keep non-locals off delicate ecosystems and restricted land. They can provide a service for tourism while still preserving the delicate ecosystem they live in.

What's more, they understand that not everyone is excited by adventure sports or extreme adventures, and thus are instead targeting the the "mild to mildly adventurous" - those who want to get outdoors, but might not be comfortable with adrenaline pumped extreme exploration.

Because they both grew up in families who went out of doors from a young age, they understand that providing a positive family vacation experience is vital for the younger generation to learn to appreciate and explore the outside world. Not only will they guide you on tours through breathteaking natural landscapes, but inspire a newfound respect and appreciation of the outside world.
