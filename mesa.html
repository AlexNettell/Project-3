Mesa Verde Trip


Adventure Level - Mild to moderate
    
Location - Meet in Durango for a 30 minute drive to Mesa Verde

Family Friendly - Yes

Season - March to October

Numbers - Up to 10 Guests

Prices
Full Day - $175pp / Youth (5-16) $95pp / under 5 $10.00

Information

Full day - includes a robust packed lunch from the local deli (vegetarian / vegan options available upon request). Return by 5pm. Bring sturdy walking shoes, sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle and your camera!

  • Mesa Verde is one of only 20 World Heritage Sites in North America.
  • Meet the Explorers Southwest Main Office at 8:00am for an 8:30am start.
  • Relax in our fully air conditioned tour bus while we take a 45 minute drive to Mesa Verde National Park on old Spanish Trail Trade Routes.
  • We will then go straight to the Mesa Verde visitor center just inside the park for an introductory park orientation.
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of mountains ranges from 4 states (Colorado, Arizona, Utah, and New Mexico) on the way to Far View Ancestral Village.
  • Explore Cliff Palace, the largest and most famous cliff dwelling in North America.
  • Kids can earn their “Junior Park Ranger Badge” while interacting with one of Mesa Verde’s park rangers.
  • Enjoy a robust lunch provided by a local deli in Durango.
  • Explore the Mesa Loop and take in a variety of archeological sites, cliff dwellings, pit house and scenic overlooks.
  • Return to Durango: 4-5pm
