What is
Explorers
Southwest?

Founded and run by Skyler and Finley, Explorers Southwest is a three season (March to October) tour company based in Durango, Colorado, USA, for the mild to mildly adventurous. Their most popular tour, coincidentally the one that started the business, is a guided jeep tour to local "ghost mining towns" in the nearby mountains between the towns of Durango and Silverton. Also on offer is a fully guided day trip tour to the Mesa Verde National Park one hour away. Lastly, they rent stand up paddle boards (SUP), fishing equipment and kayaks to use at the nearby Lake Nighthorse.

Durango Jeep
Tours

Embark on our family friendly guided jeep tour through the "ghost mining towns" scattered throughout the mountains of Durango and Silverton. Who know what you'll see along the way...

These tours are available from May to Early October. You may embark on the journey with groups up to 8 persons.

Pricing & Infomation

The trip will take you and your group half a day. For morning adventures meet at 8:00am for a 9:00am start. This journey will get you back to base for 12:00pm. For an afternoon journey we ask you to arrive at 1:00pm for a 2:00pm start which would get you back to base for around 5:00pm.

For half a day it is: Adults $60pp Youth(5-16) $25pp under 5s travel for free.

Jeep Tour: A Deeper Insight

You will get to experience numerous wildflowers and beautful rare plants, a large vareity of the local wildlife, the high-alpine meadows, avalanche gullies, impressive waterfalls and other incredible geological features.

On this adventure we will follow the La Plata River to La Plata City which also includes an old mining town which was established in 1875. On a clear day from the top of the pass you can see all the way to Cortez and Telluride.

Kids will be incredibly entertained with our "pan For Gold" scavenger hunt where they will have the chance to collect some fools gold.

Finally the roads are not well maintained so get ready for a bumpy, bouncy tour! Don't forget to bring protection from the sun, to stay hydrated, snacks for the journey and finally your camera.
